Engineering Department HR Policy
==============================

1. Vacation Policy: Engineering staff are entitled to 25 days of paid vacation per year.
2. On-call Rotation: Engineers participate in an on-call rotation and receive additional compensation.
3. Training: Engineers are eligible for up to $2,000 per year in professional development reimbursement.
4. Remote Work: Engineering staff may work remotely up to 3 days per week.
5. Equipment: The company provides high-performance laptops and monitors for all engineers.
