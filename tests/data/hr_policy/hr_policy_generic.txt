Company HR Policy
=================

1. Vacation Policy: All employees are entitled to 20 days of paid vacation per year.
2. Sick Leave: Employees may take up to 10 days of paid sick leave annually.
3. Remote Work: Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
4. Benefits: Health insurance and retirement plans are provided to all full-time employees.
5. Code of Conduct: All employees must adhere to the company's code of conduct and ethics guidelines.
